University of Edinburgh

Key facts about the city:

  • Has been called the "Athens of the North" since the 18th century
  • Home to more FTSE 100 companies than any other UK city outside of London
  • The city houses the Scottish Parliament
Key facts about the University:
  • One of Scotland's four ancient Universities
  • Part of the Russell Group
  • Second most popular university in the UK based on volume of applications
  • Ranked 16th in the 2022 QS World Rankings
  • Several members of the British royal family have studied there
Notable alumni:
  • Alexander Graham Bell - inventor
  • Charles Darwin - naturalist
  • J.M. Barrie
  • Chris Hoy - cyclist
