University of Nottingham

Key facts about the city:

  • Based in the East Midlands of England
  • Estimated population of 330,000 (as of 2020)
  • Named "Home of English Sport" in 2015
Key facts about the University:
  • Established in 1798
  • Part of the Russell Group
  • Has campuses in Malaysia and China
  • Ranked 103rd in the 2022 QS World Rankings
  • Named "University of the Year for Graduate of Employment" by The Sunday Times in 2016/2017
Notable alumni:
  • D.H Lawrence - author
  • Sir John Sawers - head of MI6
  • Ruth Wilson - actress
  • Dr Stewart Adams OBE - pharmacologist, creator of Ibuprofen
